Moon Drifter

Controllers

Desktop

`A` / `D` move left and right

`W` jump in low gravity

`E` enter a location or exit it

`R` eat a food pack

Mobile

Left / Right move across the moon

Jump thruster hop

Use enter or leave a location

Eat consume a food pack

Mission

Collect coins, food, ore, ice, and parts.

Enter the lander and outposts to explore interior levels.

Keep oxygen, hunger, and energy above zero.
